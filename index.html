Most Watched

Spirited Away

Directed by Hayao Miyazaki, "Spirited Away" is one of Studio Ghibli's most acclaimed films. It won the Academy Award for Best Animated Feature in 2003 and is celebrated for its imaginative storytelling and stunning visuals.

My Neighbor Totoro

This heartwarming film introduces Totoro, one of Studio Ghibli's most iconic characters. It follows the adventures of two sisters who discover magical creatures in the forests near their new home.

Princess Mononoke

A mature and epic tale, "Princess Mononoke" explores the conflict between nature and industrialization. It's known for its deep themes, complex characters, and breathtaking animation.
